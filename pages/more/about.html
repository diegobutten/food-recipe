Judy's Recipe & Desserts is a digital, cross-platform cooking service that helps users discover the Philippines best recipes, save and organize their cooking life, and serve as an approachable guide in the kitchen. The Food section of Filipino Times publishes feature stories, food news, recipes and restaurant reviews.



We developers make this site to help people that want to learn cooking and want to learn other food recipes. It's for those who love Filipino Foods that make them feel that they are at home whenever they are on work or overseas worker. We make this to attract people that our Filipino foods is the best for our families and friends. That we can enjoy living on loving our Filipino Culture that passes through generation.

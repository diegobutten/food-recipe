ACCESSING JUDY'S RECIPES & DESSERTS FROM YOUR DEVICES


  1. Can I view NYT Cooking on my smartphone or tablet?
  2. How do I download the NYT Cooking iOS app?
  3. Are there Android, Blackberry or Kindle apps for NYT Cooking?


Can I view NYT Cooking on my smartphone or tablet?


Yes. There is a Cooking app available for any iPad or iPhone with iOS 9.0 or later. For all other devices, you can visit our mobile site at cooking.nytimes.com


How do I download the NYT Cooking iOS app?


The NYT Cooking app is available in the App Store here. The app includes all of the recipes and collections featured on the website.


Are there Android, Blackberry or Kindle apps for NYT Cooking?


The Cooking app is currently available only on iPad and iPhone. However, the website may be accessed through any smartphone or tablet device.
